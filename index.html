Frequently Asked Questions

This is a personalized profile building, training and placement assistance program for business graduates who are aspiring for high performing industry roles like Data Analyst, CRM enabled roles, Office Management roles, etc.

Business graduates (B.Com./ BBA) who possess a good academic background, are ready to work hard to gain needed skills and have a strong desire to do great in their career.

BB programs provide you with in-depth profile analysis, personalized guidance in career selection, hands-on training in relevant skills and comprehensive placement support for high paying jobs.

As BB helps you with high-paying jobs, one is required to have skills such as communication skills, proficiency in modern office technologies, and sound academic credentials. It gives us an opportunity to understand your profile better.

If you don’t get shortlisted, our team will give you detailed feedback on how you can improve your profile and apply again.

Our methods are personalized. We focus on each student. The training approach is diverse, hands-on, interesting & targeted at aspirational jobs. We use case studies, business articles, hands-on worksheets, engaging discussions, live projects, data comprehension, individual mentoring sessions & guest speakers to deliver.

With increasing competition in every industry, managing customer relationships is increasingly becoming the most important function for organizations. Customer Success is emerging as a valuable business function that leverages advanced CRM tools (such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho) to improve business outcomes. Roles in Customer Success require the candidates to have a fundamental understanding of customer handling, sound product know-how, hands-on experience with CRM tools, and good communication skills. These roles are high-growth & high-paying ones such as Customer Success Associate, CRM Analyst, Data Analyst, Marketing Associate, Campaign Developer, Customer Delight Associate, Account Executive, etc.
