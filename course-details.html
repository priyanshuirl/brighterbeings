Admission Process

To ensure that we can effectively support you in securing high-quality job roles, we follow a thorough admission process consisting of three steps:


1. Tell us about yourself:

In the first step, you express your interest in joining the Corporate Citizenship Program at Brighter Beings by providing initial information about yourself. This includes details about your educational background, career aspirations, and reasons for joining the program. This information allows us to gain insight into your profile and assess how we can assist you.


2. Let's Meet:

Upon reviewing the information you provided, we will invite you for a personalized one-on-one discussion. During this meeting, our team will explore your unique strengths, goals, and challenges in detail. We will discuss how Brighter Beings can provide tailored support and guidance to help you achieve your desired career outcomes in Customer Success Domain.


3. Admission Decision:

Following the meeting, we will carefully evaluate whether we are well-positioned to assist you in reaching your goals. If we determine that our program aligns with your needs, we will provide details about the program deliverables, fee structure, and the specific ways in which we can support you throughout your journey. This information will give you a comprehensive understanding of the assistance we offer, the process involved, your responsibilities as a participant, and the program fees.


Once you receive this information, you will have a three-day window to make an informed decision and confirm your admission. Once you confirm, your exciting journey toward becoming a Corporate Citizen begins!
